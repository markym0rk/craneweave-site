About Craneweave

Built by founders who understand
how brands get discovered.

Craneweave was founded by two operators who spent years inside the systems that shaped brand discovery. One built a media brand that reaches tens of millions of people and counts some of the world's largest brands and organizations among its partners. One managed hundreds of millions in digital advertising at the platforms brands depended on most.

That experience gave them direct visibility into how purchase decisions form and how quickly the starting point for those decisions is shifting toward AI. Building the brand that gets found in that environment requires a team that has operated at the intersection of media, advertising, and scale. Craneweave is that team.
